BMW E30 318is

This is a 1989. 318is that is running little but sporty 1.8 liter that develops 136hp at 6000rpm. Due to this cars weight, 136hp in this car feel like a lot more. It's engine is a revolutionary masterpiece which is comparable to even today's modern engines since it's 16V.

This car had a lot of bodywork because it was really rusty, also we rebuilt the engine and swapped it, provided new wheels and tires, corrected the optics, restored suspension and lowered the car.
