Painting

What is painting?

Following the bodywork - painting is a crucial part of keeping the car looking fresh and brand new. Painting may be needed after heavy-duty bodywork or simply to eliminate various scratches that are acquired through the years.

Painting means to disassemble the chassis and carefully prepare all surface for paint to grip on it. We use variety of chemicals that protect your car from any kind of weather conditions. Our experts are here to help, feel free to contact us!
