Welcome to Autonio

~Where old meets perfect~

Who are we?

We are a car service that specialized in restoring and improving old cars - we provide services such as body work, painting, tire repair, optics, detailing and mechanic work.

Why do you need us?

If you love old cars but you're unsure on how to approach the idea of restoration or you simply can't find the time for that - we are just the service for you!

Kick back and relax because Autonio has got it all. We'll handle everything and provide daily updates so you can track the restoration progress.
