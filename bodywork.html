Bodywork

What is bodywork?

Major indicator that you are in need of bodywork can be either little things like minor dents or it can be something bigger like rust on chassis.

So to put theory to definition - bodywork is simply any work happening on the chassis. It can be classified as chassis-alignment, rust-repair or simple dent-repair. Our team has elite experts when it comes to bodywork, so don't hesitate to contact us to find out more about you specific use case!
